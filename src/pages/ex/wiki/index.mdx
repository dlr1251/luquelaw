## A legal knowledge compendium that is alive
If you find yourself struggling through the Colombian legal system. You have arrived to the right place.

This doesn't mean that you will have all your answers here, and maybe leave with even further questions. But, hopefully, you will start to learn and understand a little bit more about our legal system.

## Law is not an easy topic. 
Not here, and not anywhere, since it handles some of the most complex human situations and how the government treats the people on its territory, both nationals and foreigners.
